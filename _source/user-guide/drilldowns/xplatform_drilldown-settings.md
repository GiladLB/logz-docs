### Add a drilldown link

1. Click **+ New drilldown**.
{% include drilldowns/new_drilldown_steps.md %}

When a user clicks the link for the starting point, it directs them to the target field or dashboard.

DEEEEEELEEEETE?
2. 8 Enter a **Drilldown name** for the link.
2. 8 Select the **Start from** (source product and entity) from the list. This is the starting point for your drilldown.
3. 8 Select the **Lead to** (target product and entity) from the list. This is the target destination for your drilldown. 
4. 8 Click **Save drilldown** to confirm the new drilldown link or **Cancel**.
